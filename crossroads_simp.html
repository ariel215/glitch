The Crossroads (Simplified)

XP: 0/20

You’re coming to a crossroads — you’ll get there by the end of this quest, if you’re not there already. Both paths are covered in mist. Metaphorically, usually. You can’t see the end of either route. But you can see the cost. The costs. The cost, of going either way.
It’s pretty high.
Whichever way you go ...
You think it’s going to be high.

Major goals

  • You enter a dark forest
  • A greater power — typically, a strong Imperator or higher-ranked Excrucian — seeks you out.

Quest Flavor

  • You tentatively explore one of the paths before you
  • Someone turns away
  • Your drink or meal has gone cold, warm, or flavorless on the table
  • You remember something of what led you here
