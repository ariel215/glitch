A Nameless Light (Simplified)

XP: 0/25

Something draws you into the world. It is nameless. It is ambiguous. It may frighten you as much as it allures.

Nevertheless, it calls.

Major goals

  • You befriend a large animal
  • You hear someone's innermost pain
  • You emerge into the world with no real memory of where you've been

Quest Flavor

  • Wandering the roads of Creation
  • Remembering the forests and ruins of Ninuan
  • You visit or remember a celebration
  • Your thoughts are a-whirl
  • You cannot accept that something is as it is, or as it seems to be
